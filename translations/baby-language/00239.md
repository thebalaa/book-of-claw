## The Translation

People have different ways of identifying contacts. Some use phone numbers (like +1234567890), some use WhatsApp internal IDs, and some might use special labels starting with @lid.

This change teaches the system to understand the @lid format. It's like learning that "Mom" and "Mother" and "555-1234" all mean the same person.
