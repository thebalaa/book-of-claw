## The Translation

What if someone sends you a voice message? Claude can't "listen" to audio the way it can read text. It's like giving a book to someone who only understands spoken words!

The solution: TRANSCRIPTION! We turn the audio into text first. "Hey, what's the weather?" becomes "Hey, what's the weather?" written out. Then Claude can read it!
